ABOUT THIS OFFICE

Oldest House Operations provides discreet consulting services to organizations experiencing anomalous infrastructure, unexplained system behavior, and phenomena that fall outside standard remediation protocols.

We do not ask what the building looked like before. We do not require a complete incident history. We require only that you follow instructions exactly, remain calm, and do not use the stairs on the east side after 6pm.

Services are rendered on a case-by-case basis. Engagement terms are non-negotiable. All work is performed under a standing non-disclosure agreement with the Bureau of Unclassified Services.
